Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to RIN Shorts Engine, a private tool operated solely by its developer. The app is used only to automate posting of the owner’s own videos to TikTok. There are no other users.

Information Used

  • OAuth tokens (access and refresh) for the owner’s TikTok accounts.
  • Basic TikTok account identifiers (such as open_id).
  • Video metadata (caption, file name, scheduling info).

How Information Is Used

This information is used exclusively to authenticate the owner’s TikTok account and upload their own content. No information is collected from or shared with any third parties. No data is processed on behalf of external users.

Data Retention & Deletion

Data is retained only as long as required for posting. The developer may revoke tokens and delete data at any time.
