RIN Shorts Engine

RIN Shorts Engine is an internal automation tool created and used by a single developer for personal use. Its only purpose is to take pre-generated short videos in bulk and automatically upload them to TikTok via the official TikTok APIs.

This is not a public app, service, or product available to other users. It is strictly a private tool used by the owner to manage their own content.
